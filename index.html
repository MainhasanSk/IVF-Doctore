Medical Guidance

Understanding your fertility journey and what to expect

When Should You Consult?

  • Trying to conceive for over 12 months (or 6 months if over 35)
  • Irregular or absent menstrual cycles
  • History of multiple miscarriages
  • Known reproductive health issues (PCOS, endometriosis)
  • Male factor infertility concerns
  • Previous unsuccessful IVF attempts

First Visit Expectations

  • Comprehensive medical history review
  • Physical examination and fertility assessment
  • Personalized treatment plan discussion
  • Clear explanation of procedures and success rates
  • Transparent cost breakdown
  • All your questions answered
